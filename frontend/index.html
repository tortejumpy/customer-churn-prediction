Key Business Insights

📄
Month-to-Month = Highest Risk
42.7% of month-to-month customers churn vs only 2.8% of 2-year contract holders. Contract type is the single most powerful predictor.
⏳
New Customers at Greatest Risk
48% of customers in their first 6 months churn. Onboarding experience and early value delivery are critical retention levers.
💸
High Charges Drive Attrition
Customers paying >$95/month have a 44.6% churn rate. Price sensitivity requires targeted retention incentives for high-value customers.
🔒
Security Services Reduce Churn
Customers with Tech Support and Online Security churn significantly less. Bundling these services increases perceived value and stickiness.
💳
Electronic Check Users Churn More
Electronic check payment correlates with 45% higher churn. Migrating customers to auto-payment methods reduces friction and improves retention.
🌐
Fiber Optic Customers at Risk
Despite being a premium service, Fiber Optic customers show high churn — likely due to price. Competitive pricing and quality assurance are key.
🎯

Strategic Retention Recommendations

  • 🎁 Incentivize contract upgrades: Offer discounts or perks to month-to-month customers who upgrade to annual or biennial contracts. Even a 10% shift could dramatically reduce churn.
  • 🚀 Boost onboarding for new customers: Implement a structured 90-day onboarding journey with check-ins, tutorials, and early value demonstrations. Target 0–6 month cohort proactively.
  • 💰 Loyalty discounts for high-paying customers: Customers with >$80/month charges are at elevated risk. Offer loyalty rewards, price-lock guarantees, or service upgrades to retain them.
  • 🛡️ Bundle Tech Support + Online Security: These services strongly predict retention. Create discounted bundles and proactively offer them to customers without them, especially new subscribers.
  • 🔄 Migrate Electronic Check users to auto-pay: Offer incentives (bill credit, free month) for switching to auto-payment. This reduces churn and improves cash flow predictability.
  • 📱 Deploy real-time churn scoring: Integrate this model into your CRM to flag high-risk customers daily. Enable proactive outreach from customer success teams before churn happens.
